Running Wikipedia Miner locally involves downloading and processing a huge amount of information. We host a suite of xml-based services here at the University of Waikato that provide easier access to much of the toolkit's functionality.

Click on any of the services listed to the left to find out more about what they do, and how to use them.

Feel free to call these services directly or from within your own web applications, but bear in mind that we may restrict access if usage becomes excessive.

You can always host these same services yourself by installing your own version of Wikipedia Miner (the code to do so is included in the toolkit).
